Before you start

This is a step-by-step companion to the main Evidence Board — good for working through a contract from memory, out loud with teammates, or when you don't want to stare at a grid of toggles.

38 questions total, grouped into: Evidence (7), Common behaviors (7), Other observations (6), Advanced tells (17), and one speed question.

Only answer Yes when you're confident — a "No" or "Not sure" is treated as "no information," never as proof, except for the Evidence section where "Ruled out" is a real ✗. On Nightmare difficulty only 2 of 3 evidence are ever shown, so don't mark the third as "Ruled out" just because you haven't found it.

Your progress saves automatically — close the tab any time and pick up where you left off.
